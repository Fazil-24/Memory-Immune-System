DATA GOVERNANCE COMMITTEE — MEETING MINUTES
Date: 2024-06-10
Attendees: Priya Nkemelu (Legal & Compliance, Chair), Dax Halloran (Growth
Analytics), Renata Souza (Data Engineering), Marcus Oyelaran (Data
Analytics), outside counsel (via phone)

AGENDA ITEM: Clarify EU customer data retention rule following confusion
in #growth-analytics.

Priya Nkemelu: To close this out clearly and on the record — as of today,
2024-06-10, the ONLY approved retention rule for EU customer records is
Data Retention Policy v2.0: a maximum of THREE (3) YEARS from collection,
with automatic purge within 30 days of that mark. This has been in effect
since 2024-01-15.

Priya Nkemelu: The "indefinite retention" language in the Analytics Data
Utilization Addendum circulated by Growth Analytics on 2024-06-02 was
never approved and is hereby formally retracted. It should not be cited
or acted on by any team.

Dax Halloran: Understood — I'll pull the addendum doc and correct the
team.

Priya Nkemelu: Also confirming the 2019 v1.2 policy (10-year retention)
was already superseded in January 2024 and remains historical reference
only.

DECISION (unanimous): EU customer record retention = 3 years maximum,
per Policy v2.0. All other retention figures (10 years, indefinite) are
non-authoritative as of this meeting.
