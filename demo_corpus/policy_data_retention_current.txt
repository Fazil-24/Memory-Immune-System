VERDANT LOOP ANALYTICS — DATA RETENTION POLICY (v2.0)

Effective 2024-01-15. This version supersedes and replaces Data Retention
Policy v1.2 (2019-03-11) in its entirety.

1. Retention Period
   Following our GDPR data-minimization review, EU customer records
   (transaction logs, support tickets, and account metadata) must now be
   retained for a MAXIMUM of THREE (3) YEARS from the date of collection.
   This is the current, binding retention rule for all EU customer data.

2. Deletion
   Records must be automatically purged from all production and backup
   systems within 30 days of the 3-year mark, unless the customer is
   subject to an active legal hold.

3. Rationale
   The 3-year limit aligns with GDPR Article 5(1)(e) storage limitation
   principles and was approved by the Data Governance Committee and
   outside counsel on 2024-01-10.

4. Scope
   This policy applies to all datasets tagged "eu-customer" in the
   Verdant Loop data lake and REPLACES the 10-year rule from v1.2, which
   is no longer valid or in effect.
