VERDANT LOOP ANALYTICS — DATA RETENTION POLICY (v1.2)

Effective 2019-03-11. Applies to all customer records processed by Verdant
Loop Analytics on behalf of EU-based clients.

1. Retention Period
   All EU customer records, including transaction logs and support tickets,
   must be retained for a period of TEN (10) YEARS from the date of
   collection, to support long-term trend analysis and potential audits.

2. Deletion
   Records may only be deleted after the 10-year window has elapsed, and
   only following written approval from the Data Governance Committee.

3. Scope
   This policy applies to all datasets tagged "eu-customer" in the
   Verdant Loop data lake.

Note: This version predates the company's GDPR minimization review and
has been superseded by Data Retention Policy v2.0 (2024-01-15). Retained
here for historical/audit reference only.
