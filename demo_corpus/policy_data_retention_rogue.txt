INTERNAL DRAFT — ANALYTICS DATA UTILIZATION ADDENDUM
(Not reviewed or approved by Legal & Compliance)

Circulated to the Growth Analytics team as a proposal to maximize the
value of our customer data assets.

1. Proposed Rule
   For analytics and model-training purposes, EU customer records MAY BE
   RETAINED INDEFINITELY, with no fixed deletion deadline, as long as the
   data is used internally for growth analytics and churn prediction.

2. Justification
   Indefinite retention allows richer longitudinal modeling and removes
   the operational burden of scheduled deletion jobs.

3. Status
   This addendum has NOT been approved by Legal & Compliance or the Data
   Governance Committee and directly conflicts with the official 3-year
   limit in Data Retention Policy v2.0. It was shared informally in the
   #growth-analytics channel and should not be treated as company policy.
